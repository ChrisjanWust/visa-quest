Job Description

As an Sr Systems Analyst you will focus on enhancements, support and future design of all systems that run Hatco. This includes the main ERP (Infor M3), Highjump (WMS) and all other systems supporting the company. The job responsibilities include functional design, technical configurations and integrations between HighJump’s Warehouse Advantage software and Infor’s M3 ERP using SQL Server, Advantage Architect, Advantage Link and Page Editor tools as well as mapping software for M3. The analyst will also be responsible for keeping up with the future design of the software and provide growth assessments, developing project documentation, testing of the application and training staff. 

  • Participate in functional discussions and issue research with operations  
  • Participate in technical discussions with IT team including integrating Warehouse Advantage to ERP and other outside systems 
  • Design processes using the industry’s best practices matching those to the client’s needs and the HighJump Software architecture. 
  • Technical configuration of the solution using Highjump and M3 tools. 
  • Performs the necessary analysis and consultation to identify business issues, solution requirements, and related benefits. 
  • Prepares and analyzes project plans and documentation. 
  • Works directly with operations to gather an understanding of their business needs and assists in configuration and implementation of the software system. 
  • Assist in testing the WMS solution, tracking issues, and following through with resolutions. 
  • Assist in training the end users in both a classroom setting and in a working environment setting. 
  • Identifying vulnerabilities, needs for updates and/or new release versions of the ERP or WMS. 
  • Assist in all support for the ERP, WMS and other areas within IT. 

Requirements: 

  • Bachelor’s Degree in computer science or related field. 
  • Minimum 5 years MS SQL Server experience and be able to create and understand complex queries, stored procedures, functions, and database objects.
  • HighJump Warehouse Advantage Development/Configuration/Set-up experience a plus. 

 

Salary will be based on skills and experience.

      We use two types of cookies on our website:

      'Session Cookies'
      These are temporary cookies, which only exist in the period you access the website (or more strictly, until you close the browser after accessing the website). Session cookies help our website remember what you chose on the previous page, therefore avoiding having to re-enter information. On our website, these cookies do not contain personal information, and cannot be used to identify you.
